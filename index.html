How haksoIT works

A simple flow from client needs to useful information

The process is designed to help clients prioritise responses, track improvements and strengthen public accountability without adding unnecessary complexity.

1

Collect

Receive community inputs through forms, photos, locations and short descriptions.

2

Verify

Check location, source, evidence quality, urgency and follow-up status.

3

Analyse

Group issues by category, geography, severity and relevance to client priorities.

4

Recommend

Deliver briefs, dashboards, advisory notes and practical action points.

Example report format

Evidence that is structured enough to act on

Issue category

Road, bridge, landslide, water, sanitation, public facility or other government service.

Location

Municipality, administrative post, suco, aldeia and GPS or map location where possible.

Evidence

Photo or video, short description, date, source and urgency level.

Follow-up status

Received, verified, analysed, referred, in progress, resolved or pending.
